The Process

Simple as three steps

No accounts. No sign-ups. Just share what you know and help us reunite everyone.

01

Remember someone?

Think of a friend who left school in any class before 10th grade. You might have their number saved somewhere.

02

Fill the short form

Enter your name, the friend's name, their contact number, and the class they were in when they left. Add as many as you like.

03

We build the group

Our team reviews every submission, verifies what we can, and creates a WhatsApp group before we head off to our B.Tech journey.

Contribute

Share a contact

You can add multiple people in one submission. Even one number helps.

📬

Contact Submission

All information is kept private and used only for the group

Friends' contacts are optional — submitting your own number alone is perfectly fine.

✓

Thank you!

Your contacts have been saved. The team at Nalgonda Public School will review your submission and add verified numbers to the group.

We'll be in touch!


Student Initiative

Made with heart 🖤
for NPS alumni.

Over the years, classmates joined Nalgonda Public School, shared memories, and moved on — to different schools, different cities. This website is an attempt by three students from the 10th batch to gather every contact that time scattered, so that before we step into our B.Tech lives, we're all together in one group, one last time.
